Simple Cooking Guides

Learn how to cook easy and delicious meals at home. Perfect for Bianca!

How to Cook an Egg

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Crack the egg into the pan.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes for sunny side up, or flip for an additional 2 minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper, and enjoy ur first dish Lya.

How to Make Pancakes

  • In a bowl, mix 1 cup flour, 1 egg, 1 cup milk, and a pinch of salt.
  • Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter.
  • Pour batter onto the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes per side.
  • Top with syrup or fruit and enjoy the hardest dish you'll make.

How to Make a Mug Cake

  • In a mug, mix 1/4 cup flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, and a pinch of baking soda.
  • Add 3 tbsp milk, 2 tbsp oil, and stir until smooth.
  • Microwave for 1 minute. Let cool, then dig in!

How to Make Rice on the Stove

  • Rinse 1 cup of rice under cold water.
  • Add rice and 2 cups of water to a pot and bring to a boil.
  • Cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 18-20 minutes.
  • Fluff with a fork and eat it raw because you cant make something else!

Why You Needs to Stop Watching Brainrot

Overindulging in mindless content, often referred to as "brainrot," can have negative impacts on mental health. It can lead to issues such as:

  • Reduced attention span and focus.
  • Increased anxiety and feelings of inadequacy.
  • Reduced motivation and productivity.
  • Difficulty engaging in deep, meaningful thinking.

Taking control of what you consume online is essential for maintaining a healthy mind. Opt for educational, inspiring, or relaxing content to boost mental wellness!
